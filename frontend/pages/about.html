Our Story

Born from Obsession.
Built on Community.

We didn't set out to open a coffee shop. We set out to create a place we wished existed — where the coffee is excellent and the welcome is genuine.

The Beginning

From a Small
Roastery to a Home

Brew & Co. started in 2019 with a hand-me-down espresso machine and a lease on a tiny corner space in Church Street. Our founders, Priya and Arjun, had spent years working at specialty coffee bars across the world — from Melbourne to Lisbon — before returning to Bengaluru with one clear vision.

That vision was simple: serve coffee that's worth talking about, in a space that makes people feel like they belong. No pretension. No overpriced mediocrity. Just exceptional craft done with warmth.

Six years later, we've grown — but the philosophy hasn't changed. Every bean is still sourced with obsessive care. Every barista is trained to understand the science and the soul of what they're making. And every morning, the doors open with the same intention as day one.

Direct Trade

Where Our Coffee Comes From

🇮🇳

Coorg, Karnataka

Our house espresso blend. Washed and natural processed, with notes of jaggery and ripe plum. Sourced from a family estate we visit every harvest.

🇪🇹

Yirgacheffe, Ethiopia

Our single-origin pour-over. Floral, bright, with a jasmine finish. Directly traded with the Koke cooperative since 2021.

🇨🇴

Huila, Colombia

Our cold brew base. Honey processed for natural sweetness. Sourced from smallholder farmers in the Andes foothills.
